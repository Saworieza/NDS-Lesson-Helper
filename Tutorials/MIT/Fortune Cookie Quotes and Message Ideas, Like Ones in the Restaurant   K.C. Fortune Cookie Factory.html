Looking for Message Ideas and traditional sayings for your fortune cookies?

First, Select your occasion, and then click the red button “apply.”

Read through a few fortune cookie sayings, and if you like them, select the grey star.

The star becomes yellow and the message is added to your “favorites” list on the left side of the screen. To Remove: Click on the yellow star again to make it turn gray, and the message is removed from your list.
